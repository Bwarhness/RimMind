[h1]RimMind: AI-Powered Colony Advisor[/h1]

Transform your RimWorld colony with an intelligent AI advisor that can read, analyze, and act on your colony. Ask questions, get tactical insights, build structures, manage colonists, and let the AI guide your decisions through 51 specialized tools.

[hr][/hr]

[h2]Your Colony Deserves a Brain[/h2]

Ever wished you had a strategic advisor who actually understood your colony's situation? [b]RimMind[/b] brings cutting-edge AI directly into RimWorld, giving you an intelligent assistant that can analyze threats, optimize layouts, manage your colonists, build structures, and answer any question about your game.

This isn't just another info mod â€” it's a [b]conversational AI agent[/b] with deep game integration and 51 specialized tools that can both read and write to your colony.

[hr][/hr]

[h2]What Can RimMind Do?[/h2]

[h3]Colony Intelligence[/h3]
[list]
[*] [b]Colony Analysis[/b] - Comprehensive reports on health, wealth, mood, and vulnerabilities
[*] [b]Colonist Management[/b] - Analyze skills, relationships, health status, and work assignments
[*] [b]Medical Overview[/b] - Patients, medicine supply, available beds, doctor skill levels
[*] [b]Resource Tracking[/b] - Deep analysis of stockpiles, materials, and supplies by category
[/list]

[h3]Tactical Support[/h3]
[list]
[*] [b]Combat Analysis[/b] - Evaluate threats, raid strength, and defensive positioning
[*] [b]Map Intelligence[/b] - ASCII grid view lets the AI "see" your base layout
[*] [b]Map Search[/b] - Find colonists, enemies, ore deposits, items, buildings by type and name
[*] [b]Combat Readiness[/b] - Weapons, armor, skills assessment for each colonist
[/list]

[h3]Work & Schedule Management[/h3]
[list]
[*] [b]Work Priorities[/b] - View and set work priorities for all colonists (auto-enables manual mode)
[*] [b]Schedules[/b] - View, set, and copy hour-by-hour schedules (Sleep, Work, Anything, Joy)
[*] [b]Area Restrictions[/b] - Restrict colonists to specific allowed areas or unrestrict them
[*] [b]Production Bills[/b] - View active bills at all workbenches
[/list]

[h3]Building & Construction[/h3]
[list]
[*] [b]Place Buildings[/b] - AI places blueprints for walls, doors, furniture, workbenches, and more
[*] [b]Smart Rooms[/b] - Build complete rooms with one command (walls + door + material choice)
[*] [b]Shared Wall Detection[/b] - Adjacent rooms automatically share walls
[*] [b]Approval System[/b] - Blueprints are forbidden by default until you approve them
[*] [b]Batch Placement[/b] - Place up to 100 buildings in one call for fast construction
[*] [b]Auto-Rotation[/b] - Furniture tries all 4 rotations to find valid placement
[/list]

[h3]Zone & Area Management[/h3]
[list]
[*] [b]Create Zones[/b] - Real stockpile zones, growing zones, and planning areas
[*] [b]Growing Zones[/b] - Set crops, track growth progress, analyze soil fertility
[*] [b]Plan Markers[/b] - Place and manage visual plans using RimWorld 1.6's native plan system
[*] [b]Area Overview[/b] - View Home, Allowed, Snow Clear, Roof areas
[/list]

[h3]Colony Directives[/h3]
[list]
[*] [b]Standing Rules[/b] - Define colony personality rules the AI follows every conversation
[*] [b]Playstyle Preferences[/b] - "Melee only", "No pyromaniacs", "Granite everything"
[*] [b]Persists With Saves[/b] - Directives are saved per-colony in your save file
[/list]

[hr][/hr]

[h2]51 Specialized Tools[/h2]

The AI has access to these tools organized by category:

[code]
COLONIST (3)
  list_colonists          - All colonists: mood, job, mental state
  get_colonist_details    - Backstory, traits, skills, needs, thoughts
  get_colonist_health     - Injuries, diseases, bionics, pain level

SOCIAL (2)
  get_relationships       - Opinions, bonds, rivalries between colonists
  get_faction_relations   - Diplomacy with all factions

WORK & SCHEDULES (6)
  get_work_priorities     - Work type priority grid for all colonists
  set_work_priority       - Set a colonist's priority for any work type
  get_bills               - Production bills at workbenches
  get_schedules           - Hour-by-hour schedules for all colonists
  set_schedule            - Set schedule for a colonist at a specific hour
  copy_schedule           - Copy one colonist's schedule to another

COLONY (4)
  get_colony_overview     - Wealth, days survived, difficulty, storyteller
  get_resources           - Stockpiled items by category
  get_rooms               - Room stats: impressiveness, beauty, space
  get_stockpiles          - Zone priorities and filters

RESEARCH (3)
  get_research_status     - Current project and progress
  get_available_research  - Unlocked but incomplete projects
  get_completed_research  - All finished research

MILITARY (3)
  get_threats             - Active hostiles, raids, infestations
  get_defenses            - Turrets, traps, defensive structures
  get_combat_readiness    - Weapons, armor, combat skills per colonist

MAP (6)
  get_weather_and_season  - Weather, temperature, season, biome
  get_growing_zones       - Crops, growth progress, soil fertility
  get_power_status        - Generation, consumption, batteries
  get_map_region          - ASCII grid view of your base layout
  get_cell_details        - Drill-down: terrain, things, room info
  search_map              - Find entities by type and name filter

ANIMALS (2)
  list_animals            - Tamed animals, training, assigned master
  get_animal_details      - Health, training progress, food needs

EVENTS (2)
  get_recent_events       - Recent raids, events, letters
  get_active_alerts       - Current game alerts

MEDICAL (1)
  get_medical_overview    - Patients, medicine, beds, doctors

PLANS (3)
  place_plans             - Place visual plan markers (shapes supported)
  get_plans               - View all plans on the map
  remove_plans            - Remove plans by label, area, or all

ZONES (3)
  list_zones              - All zones with type, bounds, cell count
  create_zone             - Stockpile, growing, or planning zones
  delete_zone             - Remove zones by name

AREAS (4)
  list_areas              - All allowed areas for colonist restriction
  get_area_restrictions   - Current area assignments per colonist
  restrict_to_area        - Assign a colonist to an area
  unrestrict              - Remove area restriction from a colonist

BUILDINGS (6)
  list_buildable          - Available buildings by category with costs
  get_building_info       - Detailed building stats and requirements
  place_building          - Place individual or batch blueprints
  place_structure         - Build rooms, wall lines, wall rectangles
  remove_building         - Remove AI-proposed blueprints
  approve_buildings       - Approve blueprints for construction

DIRECTIVES (3)
  get_directives          - View colony personality rules
  add_directive           - Add a new standing rule
  remove_directive        - Remove a directive by text match
[/code]

[h2]Example Conversations[/h2]

[b]You:[/b] "What's wrong with my colony?"
[b]AI:[/b] [i]*analyzes colonists, resources, threats*[/i] "Three colonists are close to mental breaks due to low recreation. Your food supply will last 8 days. I'd recommend..."

[b]You:[/b] "Build me a 5x5 bedroom near the dining room"
[b]AI:[/b] [i]*scans map, places blueprints*[/i] "Done! Placed a granite 7x7 room with door facing the hallway. I've added a bed, end table, and dresser inside."

[b]You:[/b] "Set all colonists to sleep from 22 to 6"
[b]AI:[/b] [i]*updates schedules*[/i] "Updated schedules for 8 colonists. Hours 22-23 and 0-5 set to Sleep, remaining hours unchanged."

[b]You:[/b] "Find all steel on the map"
[b]AI:[/b] [i]*searches map*[/i] "Found 847 steel in stockpiles and 3 steel ore deposits: compacted steel at (45,120), (78,95), and (112,34)."

[b]You:[/b] "Remember: I only use granite for walls"
[b]AI:[/b] [i]*saves directive*[/i] "Got it! I've saved that as a colony directive. I'll use granite for all wall construction going forward."

[h2]Features[/h2]

[list]
[*] [b]Token Tracking[/b] - See prompt/completion token counts for each API call
[*] [b]Context Inspector[/b] - View the full system prompt, tools, and chat history sent to the AI
[*] [b]Quick Prompts[/b] - One-click test prompts for common tasks (bedroom, kitchen, killbox, etc.)
[*] [b]Colony Directives[/b] - Persistent per-save rules that shape AI behavior
[*] [b]Debug Logging[/b] - Full API request/response logs in RimMind/Logs/debug.log
[*] [b]Approval System[/b] - AI blueprints are forbidden until you approve, so nothing gets built without your say
[/list]
