[h3]Chat Window Not Opening[/h3]
[list]
[*]Check Harmony is installed and loaded before RimMind
[*]Look for errors in RimWorld's debug log (Dev Mode â†’ "~" key)

[h3]AI Responses Are Slow[/h3]
[list]

[*]Use the [b]approve_buildings[/b] tool or manually unforbid them
[*]Or use [b]auto_approve: true[/b] parameter when placing buildings
[/list]
